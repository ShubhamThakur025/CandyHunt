Welcome

Tiffi, a recent cooking college graduate, wants to whip up a delightful dessert for her guests. However, her kitchen is a mess, and she's in a hurry. To create the sweetest treats, she must match pairs of candies in limited moves. Your task is to help Tiffi find the correct candy pairs, and see how much the guests love the desserts you both prepare.
Approach her to enter the kitchen

Instructions

1. There will be 16 hidden tiles on the screen arranged in a grid in Tiffi's kitchen.
2. Click on any tile and it will reveal the candy. Click on the any other tile.
3. If you found the other tile of the pair, you'll earn a point.
4. Moves will be limited. Try to score the highest.
5. Please everyone on the table and cook even tastier the next time.
